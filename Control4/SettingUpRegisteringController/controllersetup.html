Setting Up and Registering a New Controller

  Step 1: Open Composer and look for local systems.


  Step 2: Verify that your version of Composer matches the version of the controller EXACTLY. You can download any version of Composer here: Software Downloads


  Step 3: Connect to the controller and fill out the 'Project Settings' page.


  Step 4: Select the 'Discovered' tab on the driver library and double click the controller(s) to add it to the project.


  Step 5: Once you have added all controllers, proceed to Step 6.


  Step 6: Click on 'Tools' > 'Account Services...' to register the controller.


  Step 7: Fill out the 'Add New Account' tab and then you will be prompted to register the controller. You can now update the controller as needed and finish programming the system.
